About Blues Resturant

Storytime:

Blue's Resturant started out as a dream realized by Jay Kenya in 2010. Jay set out to create a dining experience unlike any other in westlands because she had a deep passion for culinary arts and wanted to share the rich, diverse flavors of Chiken ticca. What began as a small restaurant has grown into a well-liked dining destination. We've stuck to our core values over the years: finding the best seasonal and local ingredients, carefully preparing each dish, and creating a welcoming environment where visitors are treated like family. Every meal has a backstory, and our history is proof of innumerable meals spent together, happy occasions, and the steadfast support of our beloved neighborhood.

Mission :
To delight every guest with an exceptional dining experience that transcends the ordinary.
From Dedicated Team Members :
Each one of our team is vital, from our excited line cooks and talented assistant chefs who painstakingly prepare every dish to our amiable and productive staff members, talented bartenders, and hardworking support staff. Our dedication to giving you the best dining experience possible unites us as a family. What really makes Blue's restaurant come to life every day is their combined enthusiasm and diligence.
